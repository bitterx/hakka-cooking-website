Biryani

Bee-ree-aani

“It’s like curry rice with caramelize onions. It’s very delicious rice that everyone knows about. For every family occasion we always bring it and everyone takes some!”

Ingredients

Rice and Sauce

  • 6 cups of Basmati rice
  • 1 packet of 2 Spices (Korma & Sindhi biriyani)
  • 1/2 cup Cow ghee
  • 1/2 corn oil

Chicken and Vegetables

  • 20 piece chicken legs
  • Chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup of tamarind chutney sauce
  • 5 big onions (cut into this strips
  • 3 big ground tomatoes
  • One large cauliflower

Final Sauce and Rice Yogurt

  • 1 tablespoon lime yogurt
  • 1 pinch of saffron
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • Spices like cinnamon bay leaf, cloves
  • 1 tablespoon lime yogurt (makes rice fluffier)
  • Add 1 pinch of saffron

Method

Preparing Rice and Cooking Onions

  1. Clean basmati rice with cold water.
  2. Use 1/2 cup cow ghee and 1/2 cup oil to caramelize onion at high heat.
  3. Keep aside in bowl after cooked (dark brown and crispy).

Rice Sauce and Cauliflower

  1. Add cinnamon bay leaf, cloves into 1 cup of chicken broth until aroma comes out at low heat.
  2. Strain it afterwards and leave in bowl.
  3. Chop, wash, and strain one large cauliflower.

Cooking Chicken

  1. Wash and drain chicken and put cow ghee and garlic in bowl on stove.
  2. Fry spices (masala) till it separates.
  3. Put 3 big ground tomatoes after frying spices.Add 1/2 cup of tamarind chutney sauce to mix.
  4. Take out chicken when it’s cooked (when chicken spreads into pieces) and pour sauce in separate bowl.
  5. Leave 1 cup of sauce in bowl.
  6. Cover bowl of chicken and rice.

Cooking Rice

  1. Put heat at high and add soaked rice after draining its water.
  2. Let it cook at high heat until dried up.
  3. Add rice yogurt afterwards and stir rice and add rice sauce when it sticks to bowl.
  4. Pour and mix, turn heat low, and add chicken broth if rice is not submerged.
  5. Cover with lid until there is no liquid, make sure to poke multiple holes in rice.
  6. Wait until rice becomes fluffier (like cooked rice).
  7. Can add little bit of caramelized onion oil.
  8. After it becomes fluffy, add caramelized onions and let it cook for 10 minutes.
  9. Add chicken and don’t add gravy (rice will become too salty).
  10. Add cauliflower and you’re done yay!
