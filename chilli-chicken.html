Chilli Chicken

Lat-chow-ke

“Our most special food is Chilli Chicken! Its spicy, crispy, and the best mix of Indian and Chinese food! Your brother especially loved it a lot so I made lots!”

Hakka Tofu

Ingredients

Chicken

  • 2 bags (10 pieces) of chicken leg with back
  • 1 ½ tablespoons of sweet soy-sauce
  • ½ tablespoon of seasoning soy-sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of minced garlic
  • 3 tablespoons of tapioca starch
  • 2 egg whites
  • 1 tablespoon of Cow Ghee

Vegetables (combine in one bowl)

  • 2 tablespoons of chilli oil
  • Pinch of salt
  • ½ Sweet onion
  • ½ Red pepper
  • 2 sticks of green onion
  • 2 big sweet potatoes (chopped/skinned with a pinch of salt mixed in) (leave out of bowl)

Final Sauce (mix together after)

  • 1 teaspoon of tapioca starch
  • 1 tablespoon of sweet soy-sauce and seasoning soy-sauce
  • 2 teaspoons of honey
  • Optional: 2 tablespoons of sweet or rice wine.

Method

Sweet Potatoes

  1. Cover large pan with corn oil on high heat.
  2. Put chopsticks in oil and see if bubbles form around it.
  3. Submerge sweet potatoes. Wait until they are brown and flip using silicon spatula, alternate putting potatoes in the middle of pan.
  4. Put everything in a large strainer on top of large bowl. Use mini strainer to remove the potato bits from large pan.

Cooking Chicken

  1. Mix raw minced pork with its sauce with hands.
  2. Add half-bowl of soup (homemade or canned).
  3. Get a large wok and add ¼ cup of oil using a ladle on high heat.
  4. Put chopsticks in oil and see if bubbles form around it.

Chicken and Veggies with Sauce

  1. Strain oil from chicken and use ladle to put some oil on the pan.
  2. Add veggies and mix them. Add final sauce and shut heat off.
  3. Pour fried chicken afterwards and mix everything. Make sure to dig underneath so they do not burn!
  4. Move pan away from stovetop and mix everything.
  5. Add everything to large bowl or glass container and you’re done! YAY!
